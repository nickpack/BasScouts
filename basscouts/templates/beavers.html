8th Basildon Beavers

Unfortunately due to shortage of leaders, we are unable to facilitate Beavers at this time.

The beaver section is the first section in scouting open to boys and girls age 6-8 years.

The main aim of the beaver scouts is to have fun, learn crafts, play games and learn new skills to earn badges. While they are doing this, it is a great opportunity for them to make new friends and to gain some independence, for some even staying away for the first time at camp.

All beaver scouts are encouraged to join in with new activities and to try their best in a fun and safe environment.

Please contact us for further information.
