Welcome to the 8th Basildon Scout Group.

The 8th Basildon Scout group has been going for many years and is currently one of the larger groups in Basildon offering children the chance to progress through Cubs to Scouts.

Cubs and Scouts is all about playing games, taking part in indoor and outdoor activities that challenge children, learning new skills and developing responsibilities in a safe and fun way. All of the activities are designed to suit the different age groups covered.

The three sections and are supervised by experienced leaders who have been trained and checked to work with children by the scouting origination.

Cubs and Scouts is open to any girl or boy, regardless of faith, race, culture, nationality or disability provided they are able to understand and willing to make their promise. Cubs 8 and 10½ years and Scouts 10½ and 14 years.

As a member of the 8th Basildon Scout group, your child is also a member of Scouting UK and the worldwide Scouting movement as shown by the badge they are given to wear when they are invested.
