8th Basildon Cubs.

The cub section is the second section in Scouting, is open to boy and girls age 8 – 10 ½ years.

Meetings are held every Wednesday during term time, 6.30pm – 8.00 pm.

Activities

Activities range from traditional cub scout activities and badge work, including knotting, tracking camping, backwards cooking and craft work as well as plenty of games.

Camping opportunities are twice a year:

Summer Camp
Summer camp is held some time in august which includes activities such as abseiling, Rafting, rifle shooting, backwards cooking as well as traditional camping activities and games.
Christmas camp
is usually held for one weekend at the beginning of December
Activities include Christmas craft, a full Christmas dinner, and lots of traditional fun and games.
